Workshop Wednesday Highlight

Developing Your 30-60-90 Talent Action Plan

ATTENTION PEOPLE LEADERS, THIS WORKSHOP IS FOR YOU.

Join an in-depth workshop that focuses on developing your talent action plan. Alexander Alonso, PhD SHRM-SCP, the Chief Knowledge Officer at the Society for Management (SHRM) kicks off the workshop by presenting research on the top talent acquisition and retention trends for supply chain professionals (an ISM exclusive as compared to macro trends across large employers. Joining Alexander will be a Fortune 500 Chief People Officer who will discuss these trends and real-world organizational applications. The audience will participate with a multi-generational panel representing a cross-section of procurement professionals from senior to just starting their career, who will debate their needs and perspectives for talent development.


The last portion of the workshop is focused on collaborative working time. Attendees will follow small group exercises to facilitate discussions on key findings, ideas and sharing solutions to key challenges. Using the workshop exercises and tools, each attendee will leave with their own personal talent action plan.

This workshop is among three half-day workshops included with your conference registration. Register by February 10th to receive best pricing.
